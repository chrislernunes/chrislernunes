## Projects

### **Nimbus Public**
A comprehensive options pricing and market making engine that implements Black‑Scholes analytics, full second‑order Greeks, SABR model calibration via Levenberg–Marquardt, and arbitrage‑free SVI implied volatility surface construction. Designed for research and strategy prototyping in derivatives markets.

### **_edgeflow**
A real‑time, low‑latency market microstructure engine for Binance spot markets. It streams live order book data, extracts microstructure features, and computes composite alpha signals — a foundation for live automated trading or signal research.

### **Pluto**
A Python‑based backtesting engine for systematic traders and researchers. Focused on reproducible quantitative experiments, clean architecture, and fast iteration, Pluto supports intraday and multi‑strategy testing workflows.

### **Volatility‑arbitrage**
A strategy research project exploring dispersion trading — capturing the premium between index implied volatility and its constituents’ weighted implied volatilities. Includes conceptual analysis and data exploration to assess mean‑reversion of implied correlation.
